About Us

At NewSpace360, we bring a practical yet innovative approach to the construction industry. With a background in both photography and electrical work, Rodrigo Mozzer understands the intricate balance between creativity and technicality. Having spent five years as an electrician, he recognizes the challenges contractors face in visualizing their projects. Our mission is to provide you with a solution that eliminates uncertainty and guesswork. By utilizing 3D cameras and aerial drones, we capture every detail of your projects, empowering you with a clear and comprehensive view of your work-in-progress. Our commitment is rooted in simplifying your decision-making process, enhancing client communication, and optimizing project management. With NewSpace360, you can rely on a practical toolset that turns raw spaces into tangible visions. Join us in redefining how you see and shape the future of construction.
